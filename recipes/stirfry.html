Stir Fry

Description

A delicious stir fry dish with chicken, veggies, and a sweet and savory sauce. This dish can have a mix of all kinds of veggies of your choosing.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 servings of udon noodles
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 bag of broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • White sesame seeds, to taste

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of water and begin cooking the noodles.
  2. Dice the onion and cook it in a large pan with the oilive oil.
  3. While the onions are cooking, dice up the chicken. Season with salt and pepper, and add to the pan until cooked through.
  4. Add in the broccoli or any other vegetables.
  5. Make the sauce in a bowl. Stir and add to the pan.
  6. Drain the noodles and combine them with the rest of the dish.
  7. Season with sesame seeds and enjoy!
