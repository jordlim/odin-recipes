Grilled Cheese

Description

This is how to make a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick white bread
  • 1/3 white cheddar
  • 1/3 yellow cheddar
  • 1/3 American cheese
  • 1 slice of butter

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in the pan
  2. Place the cheese on one slice of bread. Place the other slice on top.
  3. Grill it.
